    Tips

    • Description

    Introduction

    • Thread Specifications: M (Metric), PG, G (PF), NPT
    • Material: Brass / Stainless Steel
    • Protection Level: IP68
    • Working Temperature: Static -40℃ to 100℃, or can heat up to 120℃ instantly. (Can be customized from -60℃ to 220℃)
    • Product Features: Special clamping jaw and clamping ring design, large clamping cable range, strong tensile strength, waterproof, dustproof, salt-resistant, weak acid, alcohol, grease and general solvents
    • Support non-standard customization

    Structure

    • Parts A, C, and F are made of Nickel-plated brass / Stainless Steel.

    • Part D is made of nylon.

    • Parts B and E are made of nitrile rubber.

      (Can be customized high temperature resistant silica gel, acid and alkali resistant fluorine rubber)
